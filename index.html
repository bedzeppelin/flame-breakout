FLAME BREAKOUT ROOM

A FLAME STEM Challenge
Deep beneath camp lies the Ember Vault, where the very first campers sealed away the Eternal Flame long ago. Something has disturbed it — the vault is heating up, and three ancient seals must be found, uncovered, and decoded before it overflows.
Look closely. The clues aren't always in plain sight — some are hidden behind the flames themselves. Search, decode, and speak the calming chant before time runs out. You have 8 minutes.
Everyone starts on the same “Go” so the clock is fair across the room.
08:00

Seal 1 — The Ember Runes

One of these flames is hiding a secret panel. Click around to find it.
You found it! A rune panel glows behind the flame:
01100 01100 00001 01101 00101
Five groups = a 5-letter word about what the vault is protecting.

Seal 2 — The Cinder Signal

Only one of these torches will actually catch. Click one to try lighting the beacon.
The beacon flares to life, flashing and beeping a pattern on repeat.
Written pattern: −···   ·−··   ·−   −−··   · (5 letters, spells what the vault is about to do).

Seal 3 — The Scorched Scroll

A row of wax seals sits on the scroll. Only one hasn't fully hardened — click to break it open.
The wax cracks open, revealing a shift key burned into the scroll:
SHIFT KEY: 4
MKRMXI
M shifted back 4 is I. Do that for every letter. It's a 6-letter word meaning "to light."

Speak the Calming Chant

You've uncovered all 3 words. Enter them in the order you found them, separated by spaces, to calm the Eternal Flame.

VAULT CALMED

The Eternal Flame settles back to a steady glow. Well found, well decoded.

TIME'S UP

The vault holds for now, but the chant wasn't finished — nice searching anyway. Regroup for the debrief.
